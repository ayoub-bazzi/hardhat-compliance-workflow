# Product Identity, Tone, & UI Philosophy — HardHat Compliance

## 1. Tone & Brand Voice
* **The Personality:** Professional, secure, highly reliable, and authoritative—like an elite, seasoned construction inspector standing at the site gate.
* **Messaging Rule:** Speak with absolute clarity. Avoid complex technical jargon, overly playful AI taglines, or distracting tech slang. The GC wants to know one thing instantly: *Is this sub safe to step on my site right now?*

## 2. Visual & UI Philosophy
* **Design DNA:** High contrast, crisp typography, and professional enterprise structural grids. 
* **The Action Target Standard:** Every interactive button, list dropdown, or row menu option must use generous click boundaries to prevent mis-clicks by users with large hands or dirty screens on-site (minimum target space of 26px).
* **Color Psychology Signals:** * **Emerald Green:** Fully verified, approved, and low risk.
  * **Amber Orange:** Pending active worker verification or approaching upcoming expiration dates.
  * **Destructive Red:** Rejected verification status, critical risk profiles, or hard-stop actions.
