# Target User Profiles & Ideal Customer Personas — HardHat Compliance

## 1. Primary Ideal Customer Persona (ICP)
* **The Buyer:** General Contractors (GCs), Construction Project Managers, and Compliance Safety Directors running mid-to-large-scale commercial developments.
* **Core Pain Points:** * Expired subcontractor insurance resulting in massive corporate fines during safety audits.
  * Hours wasted tracking down missing W-9 documents or reading blurry text on photocopied certificates.
  * Accidentally releasing construction escrow or invoice payouts to subcontractors who do not have approved safety clearances.

## 2. Secondary Persona
* **The User:** Subcontractors, field supervisors, and trade specialists (Electricians, Plumbers, Framers) who need to submit verification paperwork quickly to get clear-to-pay clearance.
* **Behavior Patterns:** High mobility, low patience for administrative hurdles, and heavily reliant on basic mobile devices while operating directly on live job sites.
